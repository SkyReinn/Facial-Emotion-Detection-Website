How it works

Facial recognition is a technology used to identify or verify individuals from images or videos of their faces. It uses algorithms to analyze and compare specific facial features from an image with a database of known faces, in order to identify an individual or confirm their identity. It can also be used to identify emotions of the individual such as happy, sad, or angry.
